Tower Stack

Introduction

Tower stacking game re-created in Unity. Play online!

Experience the thrill of building a tower and test your precision in this addictive puzzle game. What sets this game apart is its visual experience. A dynamic gradient color system has been implemented, giving each block a vibrant and distinct hue. As you skillfully stack block upon block, an explosion of colors fills the screen, creating a mesmerizing display. Keep a streak of perfect block placement to grow back the size of your block!

The excitement doesn't end there. Every new round introduces a new and randomly selected background color, keeping you visually engaged. The combination of vibrant block colors and dynamic backgrounds creates a visually stunning experience.

And let's not forget the competitive aspect! The game saves the highest score achieved, allowing you to compete against yourself and friends.

Projects

Dream Wars

3D car game made in UE4. Drive around in this dreamy field while shooting down all turrets before time runs out! All car mechanics are built from scratch and programmed in C++. The AI for turrets uses blueprints.

Push and Match

3D puzzle game made in Unity for a school game jam. Push the boxes on the tiles to match the pattern on the wall! The puzzles are auto-generated and randomized. The tiles on the floor get generated and placed in the proper spots automatically. I used different physics material and tweaked colliders to achieve a smoother result when pushing boxes.

The Infinite Run

2D side scroller made in C++, using SDL. This entire game was made in C++ from scratch. It has a simple engine, state manager, animation, player movement and physics, save and load, and randomized trap spawner. I created various sets of traps and the program randomly chooses from the possible combinations.

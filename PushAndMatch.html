School Game Jam

Introduction

Made in Unity for school's game jam with a puzzle theme. Play online! https://m3hrara.itch.io/pushmatch

Use the mouse to look around and WASD to move Mr Foxie. Push the boxes onto the correct slates to match the pattern on the wall.

Each round there is a new pattern randomly generated on the wall. The position of the boxes reset to their initial spots. There is colliders on the slates to detect collision with the boxes.

It was a great learning experience to generate the patterns on the wall and have them placed inside a grid all automatically. Another challenging aspect that I overcame was detecting if all the boxes are on the right spot and match the pattern!
